About Me

For the past 14 years, I have worked primarily with Ruby on Rails in several startups and companies. Currently, as a systems engineer at Clearhaus, managing and developing payments systems. Before that, senior software engineer at Peanut, building APIs for scaling a social network for women.

I've also worked at Peanut (social network), GoMore (ride-sharing), Skyscanner (travel planning), HouseTrip (holiday rentals), Evently (event management).

My drive comes from delivering value to the business and enjoyment to its users and customers. Besides having solid experience with web applications, I also have experience with marketing automation, finance, analytics, mobile development, DevOps and leading small teams.
