About Me

For the past 12 years, I have worked primarily with Ruby on Rails in several startups and companies. Currently, as a senior software engineer at Peanut, building APIs for scaling a social network for women. Before that as CTO and co-founder building a platform for ordering and delivering graphic designs.

I've worked also at GoMore (ride-sharing), Skyscanner (travel planning), HouseTrip (holiday rentals), Evently (event management).

My drive comes from delivering value to the business and enjoyment to its users and customers. Besides having solid experience with web applications, I also have experience with marketing automation, finance, analytics, mobile development, DevOps and leading small teams.
